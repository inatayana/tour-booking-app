### Page Purpose & UI Elements

#### HOME (baligo.tours/)

- **Purpose:** First impression, value prop, encourage booking
- **UI Elements:** Hero section, featured experiences carousel, destinations showcase, testimonials, CTA buttons
- **CTAs:** "Book an Experience" (primary), "Explore Destinations" (secondary)

#### BALI EXPERIENCE (baligo.tours/bali-experience/)

- **Purpose:** Hub for all experience categories
- **UI Elements:** Category grid, featured products per category, filter/search
- **CTAs:** "Explore [Category]" buttons

#### PRODUCT DETAIL (baligo.tours/bali-experience/[category]/[slug])

- **Purpose:** Drive booking for individual product
- **UI Elements:** Hero image, gallery slider, itinerary, inclusions, pricing, reviews, booking form
- **CTAs:** "Book Now", "Add to Wishlist"

#### DESTINATIONS (baligo.tours/destinations/)

- **Purpose:** Inspire travel and showcase Bali regions
- **UI Elements:** Destination cards, featured attractions, gallery per destination
- **CTAs:** "Explore Experiences in [Destination]"

#### DESTINATION DETAIL (baligo.tours/destinations/[slug])

- **Purpose:** Deep-dive into region, showcase local attractions
- **UI Elements:** Hero image, overview, attractions list, hidden gems, gallery, related experiences
- **CTAs:** "Book Experience in [Destination]"

#### ABOUT (baligo.tours/about/)

- **Purpose:** Build trust, explain mission, show local expertise
- **UI Elements:** Brand story, mission/vision, team photos, sustainability commitment, testimonials
- **CTAs:** "Contact Us for Custom Experiences"

#### CONTACT (baligo.tours/contact/)

- **Purpose:** Enable multiple communication channels
- **UI Elements:** Contact form, WhatsApp link, email, phone, office location (map)
- **CTAs:** "Send Message", "Chat on WhatsApp"

## 2. HOMEPAGE CONTENT STRATEGY

### Hero Section Strategy

- **Layout:** Full-width image background with overlay text
- **Tone:** Warm, welcoming, local-expert
- **Copy Goals:** Immediate value prop + emotional connection + clear CTA
- **Image Style:** Sunrise/golden hour, local guide, authentic travelers, high production quality

### Content Goals

1. Stop the scroll (compelling headline)
2. Build desire (benefit subheading)
3. Establish credibility (social proof, local expert positioning)
4. Drive action (clear, urgent CTA)

## 3. PRODUCT PAGE TEMPLATE REQUIREMENTS

### Per-Product Information

- **Title** (max 70 chars)
- **Category** (Tours/Adventure/Experience/Spirituality/Services)
- **Duration** (e.g., "4 hours", "Full day")
- **Group Size** (e.g., "2-8 people")
- **Difficulty Level** (Easy/Moderate/Challenging)
- **Price** (starting price + dynamic pricing by group size & date)

### Content Blocks

1. Overview (80-120 words)
2. Highlights (3-5 bullet points)
3. Full Description (150-200 words)
4. Itinerary (step-by-step if applicable)
5. Included/Excluded
6. Know Before You Go (tips, restrictions)
7. What to Bring (packing list)
8. Booking Form (interactive)
9. Reviews (testimonials + star rating)
10. Related Experiences (recommendations)

### UX Elements

- **Image Gallery:** 6-8 professional images
- **Booking Widget:** Date picker, time selector, participant counter, dynamic price, "Book Now" button
- **Testimonials:** 3-4 reviews with photos + names
- **FAQ:** 3-5 common questions
- **Share Buttons:** Social sharing options

## 4. DESTINATIONS STRATEGY

### Per-Destination Content

- **Overview:** What makes this place special (150-200 words)
- **Tourist Attractions:** 5-7 main points of interest
- **Hidden Gems:** 3-4 lesser-known spots (local expertise)
- **Gallery:** 5 high-quality images (drone, landscape, cultural)
- **Related Experiences:** Links to 4-6 relevant tours/activities
- **Best Time to Visit:** When to go
- **How to Get There:** From airport/main areas

### Destinations Covered

1. **Ubud** - Cultural heart, rice terraces, arts
2. **Uluwatu** - Clifftop temples, surfing, sunset views
3. **Sanur** - Beach town, water sports, local market
4. **East Bali** - Mountains, temples, less crowded
5. **North Bali** - Waterfalls, adventure, quiet beaches

## 7. SEO METADATA STRATEGY

### Per-Page Elements

- **Meta Title:** 50-60 characters (includes main keyword)
- **Meta Description:** 150-155 characters (benefit-focused)
- **Long-tail Keywords:** 5 variations per page
- **Slug:** URL-friendly, keyword-rich, lowercase, hyphens
- **Heading Structure:** H1 (page title), H2 (sections), H3 (subsections)
- **Internal Linking:** 3-5 contextual links to related pages
- **Image Alt Text:** Descriptive, includes keywords where natural

### Keyword Strategy

- **Primary:** "Bali tours", "Bali experiences", "things to do in Bali"
- **Activity-Specific:** "[activity] Bali", "[activity] tour near me"
- **Destination-Specific:** "Ubud tours", "Uluwatu temple visit", "Sanur beaches"
- **Long-tail:** "best authentic cultural tours in Bali", "sustainable eco-tourism Bali"

## 9. BOOKING FLOW

### Step-by-Step

1. **Product Page** → Customer views experience details
2. **Date Selection** → Calendar picker (available dates only)
3. **Time Selection** → Available time slots
4. **Participant Count** → Number selector (min/max based on product)
5. **Price Calculation** → Dynamic pricing displayed real-time
6. **Book Now** → Proceed to checkout
7. **Checkout Page** → Review booking, enter personal info
8. **Payment Selection** → Credit card, PayPal, bank transfer options
9. **Payment Processing** → Secure payment gateway
10. **Confirmation** → Digital voucher + booking details emailed

### Post-Booking

- **Customer:** Receives digital voucher, booking details, guide contact info
- **Admin:** Dashboard notification, customer contact info, booking history
- **Guide:** Notified of confirmed booking with customer details
