## IMAGE ASSET MANAGEMENT

### File Naming Convention

- Format: `[category]-[subject]-[type]-[date].jpg`
- Example: `ubud-rice-terraces-hero-2024.jpg`
- Keep lowercase, use hyphens, include year for version control

### Image Optimization

- **Hero images:** 1200px width, max 150KB
- **Category headers:** 1200x600px, max 100KB
- **Thumbnails:** 400x300px, max 50KB
- **Portrait images:** 400x400px, max 60KB
- Compress using TinyPNG or similar
- Use WebP format for modern browsers, fallback to JPG

### Stock Photo Alternatives

If custom photography not available:

- Unsplash: bali, indonesia, travel
- Pexels: bali temples, waterfalls, yoga
- Pixabay: sunset, adventure, nature
- Avoid: overly staged, too polished (inconsistent with "local" brand)
- Prioritize: authentic, candid, culturally respectful images

### AI Generation Guidelines

If using AI image generators (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion):

- Always verify cultural accuracy
- Avoid stereotypes or caricatures
- Include diverse representation
- Ensure professional quality and composition
- Use prompts with specific details (dimensions, lighting, mood)
- Have human review before publishing

## SEASONAL IMAGE UPDATES

### Dry Season (April-October)

- Emphasize clear blue skies, sunshine
- Show optimal visibility
- Highlight water sports and beach activities
- Use bright, warm lighting

### Wet Season (November-March)

- Dramatic storm skies, mood lighting
- Lush green landscapes (more vibrant)
- Waterfalls at peak flow
- Moody, atmospheric tones

## VISUAL BRAND CONSISTENCY

### Color Palette Application

- Warm Oranges/Golds (#FF8C42, #FFB700) – Used in hero images, sunset, cultural moments
- Ocean Blues (#0099CC, #00B4D8) – Water activities, sky, calming elements
- Jungle Greens (#2D6A4F, #40916C) – Nature, adventure, rice paddies
- Gold/Warm Tones (#D4A574) – Balinese cultural, temples, spirituality
- Earth Browns (#8B7355) – Authenticity, tradition, craftsmanship

### Photography Style

- **Overall:** Documentary travel photography with authentic moments
- **Lighting:** Golden hour preferred, natural light prioritized
- **Composition:** Wide landscapes, intimate close-ups, action shots
- **Mood:** Welcoming, adventurous, spiritual, joyful
- **Authenticity:** Real people (not generic stock), genuine emotions, unfiltered moments
